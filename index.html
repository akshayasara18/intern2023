About Us

CINE WORLD!!

Cine Suvai in Madurai is a cinema-themed restaurant that opened in 2019, initially as a rebrand of the Bell Tiffin Centre. It's known for its unique decor inspired by popular Tamil movie scenes and iconic movie props. The restaurant features a 300-seater capacity and is designed to evoke the feeling of old Art Deco theaters.

The restaurant is decorated with props, names, and elements from Kollywood movies, including iconic scenes and movie posters.It offers a fun and engaging experience for foodies and movie buffs, allowing them to dine in a movie-themed environment.

Service staff may be dressed in costumes of celebrated movie characters, further enhancing the theme.Besides the cinema theme, Cine Suvai serves a variety of food, including South Indian curries, biryanis, barbecue, and Chinese cuisine.Cine Suvai was relaunched in 2019 by Vichitra, who decided to transform the existing Bell Tiffin Centre into a cinema-themed restaurant. The restaurant was inaugurated by Mr. C.R. Venkatesh and Mrs. Poornima Venkatesh
